I am a law lecturer and impact lead at the University of Aberdeen and a consultant to governments and international organizations. I specialise in competition law and policy, IT law and data science. Previously, I was an assistant professor at KIMEP University, a visiting fellow at Cambridge, and a visiting professor at IUC (Turin) and Trinity College Dublin. I've also been a research fellow at UCL, Max Planck Institute, and Europa-Kolleg, Hamburg. I've published extensively on competition law, regulation of digital platforms, and comparative law. I am a licensed attorney in Italy.
